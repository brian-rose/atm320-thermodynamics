AATM 320: Atmospheric Thermodynamics

About

This is the home page for the Fall 2023 edition of the course AATM 320: Atmospheric Thermodynamics.

This is a required junior-level course in the Atmospheric Science BS offered by the Department of Atmospheric & Environmental Sciences at the University at Albany.

The instructor (and author of these pages) is Brian Rose.

How to use this site

Our course in Fall 2023 closely follows the book A First Course in Atmospheric Thermodynamics by Grant W. Petty, Sundog Publishing, 2008. Material on this site is meant to serve as companion material to our lectures and readings from Petty’s book.
